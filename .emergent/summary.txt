**what currently exists?**
A full-stack Carbon Footprint Calculator application built with React, FastAPI, and MongoDB. The application supports multi-tenancy, JWT authentication, and is bilingual (FR/DE). It features a dashboard, data entry page, and company info page.

Major features implemented:
-   **Advanced Data Entry:** A multi-step modal for guided data entry, and a detailed tabular view for reviewing/editing/deleting existing entries.
-   **Product Lifecycle Management:** A complete system for defining complex products, including their composition, transformation energy, usage emissions, and end-of-life treatment. A wizard guides users through product creation. Sales can be recorded, which automatically generates the corresponding Scope 3 emissions activities.
-   **Fiscal Year Management:** Backend and frontend infrastructure for creating, viewing, closing, and rectifying fiscal years. Data entry is scoped to the selected fiscal year.
